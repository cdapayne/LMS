Getting Started

Log in with your username and password. Update your profile and confirm your contact information so teachers can reach you.

Dashboard

The dashboard summarizes your enrolled classes, upcoming deadlines and announcements from teachers and administrators.

Classes & Materials

Open a class to view lessons, slides and videos. Download resources for offline study and check the schedule for meeting times.

Assignments & Tests

  • Submit Work: upload documents or type responses directly in the browser.
  • Take Tests: complete quizzes and exams within the time limit. Review your answers before submitting.
  • Feedback: read teacher comments to improve future work.

Communication

Stay informed by reading announcements, participating in discussions and checking your email for system messages. Use the messaging tools to ask questions or collaborate with classmates.

Grades & Progress

View grades and attendance on your dashboard. Track completion status for each assignment and download reports if available.

Calendar & Events

Access the calendar to see upcoming classes and school events. RSVP when required so organizers know you are attending.

Support

If you encounter problems, consult your teacher or the system administrator. Keeping your browser updated and clearing cache often resolves common issues.

We wish you success in your studies!
