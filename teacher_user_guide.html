Teacher User Guide

This guide describes how teachers deliver courses and support students in the learning management system.

Dashboard

The dashboard lists your active and upcoming classes along with recent announcements. Use the quick links to jump directly to grading tasks or discussions.

Class Management

Open a class from the dashboard or the class list to manage enrollment and schedule.

  • Roster: view enrolled students, remove participants or send direct emails.
  • Schedule: edit meeting times and publish updates for students.
  • Discussions: facilitate class conversations and moderate posts.

Course Materials

Upload lesson files, slides and videos to support instruction. Group materials by topic to help students navigate content.

Assessments & Grading

  • Create Tests: build quizzes and exams with multiple question types.
  • Grade Submissions: evaluate student work, provide feedback and release grades when ready.
  • Gradebook: review overall progress and export grades for reporting.

Communication

Keep students informed through announcements, messaging and discussions. Encourage questions and respond promptly to maintain engagement.

Reports

Access class reports to track completion rates and identify students needing support. Use data to adjust instruction and provide targeted feedback.

Account Settings

Update your profile, change your password and manage notification preferences. Keeping your information current ensures you receive important alerts.

For additional assistance, contact your system administrator.
